    • COMPOSITE POSTER DESIGN

    The poster has a symmetrical composition with a vibrant color palette, inspired by Wes Anderson's distinct visual style. The film's title is Bajirao Mastani, and it includes the names of the cast members' names in Anderson-inspired alignment. Behind the actress' image is a picture of the Taj Mahal, the epitome of love, which is closely linked to the theme of Bajirao Mastani.

    MAGAZINE POSTER

    The poster design is inspired by the classic sci-fi genre, which combines elements of fantasy and reality to create a sense of wonder and adventure. The subtitles are used in such a way to generate the curiosity among the indivuals. The poster uses a bright and colorful scheme that contrasts with the dark background of space, to create a visual impact and appeal.

    SONG POSTER DESIGN

    This visual piece explores the concept of connection and shared experiences through the universal symbols of hands, eyes, and the celestial elements. The juxtaposition of different languages and scripts emphasizes the global nature of human connection, transcending boundaries. The title "ये रातें ये मौसम" translates to "These Nights, These Seasons," inviting contemplation of the cyclical nature of time and the emotions it evokes. The overall aesthetic is inspired by Wes Anderson's signature style, characterized by symmetry, pastel colors, and whimsical details.

    VINYL CD DESIGN

    The artwork reimagines Vincent van Gogh with a modern, minimalist twist. Using grayscale tones, bold lines, and shadow overlays, it portrays a fusion of classic artistry and contemporary style. The sunglasses symbolize a rebellious, modern-day persona, contrasting his historic identity while maintaining an air of mystery and artistic brilliance.
